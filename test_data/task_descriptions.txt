The following is the list of tasks and their descriptions.
- StartOrBuildOrder: When the buyer expresses their intention to start and order or purchase an item or add items to existing order.  Only when the buyer has confirmed that they are done adding things to their cart and would like to proceed to purchase do we move on to the next task of FinalizeOrder.
- FinalizeOrder: If the buyer is done adding things to their cart and has affirmed the offer to proceed with purchase. We first send them a signup link to provide us with their shipping and billing information. Once they have filled the form online, we ask for a confirmation again based on the total price of their cart including shipping. If they affirm, we place the order and the task is finished.
- RecommendProduct: When the buyer is asking us for a recommendation on products.
- UpdateAccountDetails: The buyer wants to update their address or just the address for delivery or credit card details.
- AnswerQuestionAboutOrder: The buyer has a question about the order about to be placed or being placed.
- AnswerQuestionAboutProduct: The buyer has a question about a product or few products.
- AnswerQuestionAboutSellerorService: The buyer has a question about the seller or its offerings or they are confused about how to use the service and get the discount.
- ResolveOrderIssue: The buyer has an issue withe their recent order.
- GiveOrderStatus: The buyer wants to know the status of their most recent order.
- CancelOrder: The buyer wants to cancel the order.
- ReturnOrder: The buyer wants to return the order.
- None: The buyer is not really expecting us to do anything or say anything in response. or we are not sure which of the above tasks the buyer wants to accomplish.
